Teaching methodology

In our work we use communicative
teaching method, the essence of which is
creating terms of use in class
means of a foreign language by students,
motivation for spontaneous foreign speech.
Simultaneous learning and development
all components of language competence, namely:
development of phonetic skills (staging
pronunciation), listening skills (understanding
foreign speech by ear) and reading skills,
constant replenishment of vocabulary,
grammar teaching.

In the classroom, we use authentic teaching materials.
manuals from Oxfor University Press,
Cambridge University Press, MacMillan Puplishers Ltd
and for the German language Hueber, Duden,
Langenscheidt and others.
